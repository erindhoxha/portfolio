Tourism New Zealand

Project brief

Tourism New Zealand works with the tourism industry to positively promote New Zealand internationally. They have a new campaign that is aimed at visitors staying for short periods, booking their own accommodation and arranging their own transport. My task was to create a single page web application that allows users to input information, validate the information and provide meaningful feedback when validation has failed or passed. Display accommodation or transport options to the user based the information entered. Calculate and display either accommodation or transport costs to the user. Tourism New Zealand want the app to have its own distinctive look and feel (whilst still remaining cohesive with the Tourism New Zealand brand).
